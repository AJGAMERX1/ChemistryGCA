Activation Energy
	Minimum energy required to start a reaction; the peak in an energy diagram
	Energy barrier shown on a reaction graph
	Activated Complex
	Temporary unstable arrangement of atoms at highest energy
	Peak of a reaction pathway diagram


	Catalyst
	Substance that speeds up a reaction without being consumed
	Platinum used in catalytic converters


	Enzyme
	Biological catalyst
	Amylase breaking down starch
	Inhibitor
	Substance that slows a reaction


	A substance blocking an enzyme’s active site
	Rate-Determining Step
	Slowest step in a mechanism
	Step 2 in a mechanism labeled “slow”


	Intermediate
	Substance formed and then used up
	A molecule produced in step 1 and used in step 2
	Adsorption
	Sticking to a surface
	Gas molecules sticking to metal surface
	Absorption
	Dissolving throughout a material 
	Sugar dissolving in water
